Testing the Center Pivot Irrigation Planning Tool

To test the application:

  1. Open index.html in your browser
  2. Click "Import Land Polygon" to load a KML or GeoJSON file
  3. Click "Draw Circle Pivot" and click on the map to add full circles
  4. Click "Draw Semi-Circle Pivot" and click on the map to add semi-circles
  5. Select a pivot by clicking on it
  6. Configure tower settings in the sidebar
  7. Click "Apply Towers" to add towers to the selected pivot
  8. Export your design as KML or PDF
